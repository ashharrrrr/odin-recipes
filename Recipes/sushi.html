Sushi

Description

To get started, you need the right kind of seaweed. Spread the seaweed with a layer of prepared sushi rice and flatten gently with a rice paddle.

Here is how I cut salmon for my sushi rolls. First, cut straight down through the filet, then cut that piece in half through the center so you have a small strip Place your desired ingredients onto the rice. I’ve got salmon, avocado, and cream cheese. Roll it up tightly, using a bamboo mat.Then use a sharp knife to cut the sushi roll into pieces. I don’t find it particularly better to use a serrated knife. Just make sure to use a sharp one!

Ingredients

  • 6 sheets sushi seaweed
  • 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  • 1/2 lb sushi-grade raw salmon or desired raw fish of choice
  • 4 oz cream cheese sliced into strips
  • 1 avocado
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Place the seaweed on a bamboo mat, then cover the sheet of seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice. Smooth gently with the rice paddle.
  2. Layer salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on the rice, and roll it up tightly. Slice with a sharp knife, and enjoy with soy sauce.
